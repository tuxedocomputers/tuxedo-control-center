To configure your webcams, you'll need to plug in an external webcam or turn on the internal webcam. Once you've done that, you can press reload and you'll be presented with a configuration menu. If you've previously disabled the internal webcam in the profile settings, you'll need to re-enable it in order to configure it. Some laptops also allow you to toggle the webcam quickly with a keyboard shortcut.
